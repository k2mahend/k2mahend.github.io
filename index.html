Work Experience

Stratos Solutions

I worked at Stratos Solutions as a tax associate. I mainly focused on analyzing transactions within large companies for probable tax recoveries. In addition to tax recovery, I also worked on some customs duties reclassification and accounts payable recoveries.

To be more specific, the clients I worked on for tax recovery ranged from companies in the energy sector and real estate to retail companies. I presented the clients with recovery reports illustrating the transactions along with the accompanying proof. I led a team on analyzing utility bills for our real estate client, recovering the client upwards of $3 million dollars.

Royal Bank of Canada

At RBC, I worked as a business systems analyst on the risk management team under Global Hosting Services. With a fellow colleaguge, we developed an enterprise governance model in response to an L2 audit issue. This required coordination and co-operation between several parts of the bank including asset management, application support, server administration, etc. The model made, ensures applications are following company policy and is accompanied with a reporting procedure.

Every week, I created risk reports which were sent to senior management informing them of organizational complaince, audit timelines, employee deviances, anomalies, etc. I made a VBA macro that was able to process the raw data, filter out the relevant information pertaining to senior management, sort it in order of relevance and email the necessary parties automatically. This cut down the time it took to make reports by over 80%.

I also worked as part of the bank's legacy machine team; this included the Tandem and the iSeries machines. Using VBA, I made a macro that would show potential risks within the machines and email selected parts of the reports to the registered owner. Additionally, I made scripts that would make sure user permissions were not modified and a script that would help reduce downtime in disaster recoveries.
